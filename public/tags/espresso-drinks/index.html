Brown Butter Latte Recipe: Nutty, Rich, and Incredibly Easy

What Is a Brown Butter Latte? A brown butter latte is a specialty espresso drink built around beurre noisette — butter cooked past the melting point until the milk solids turn golden and develop deep, nutty, toasty flavor. That browned butter is whisked into hot espresso until it emulsifies, then topped with steamed milk. The result is unlike anything else in the latte world: a drink with the creamy body of a standard latte, but with haunting notes of toasted hazelnuts, caramel, and brown sugar woven through the espresso.
