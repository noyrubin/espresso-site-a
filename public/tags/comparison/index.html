Cold Brew vs Iced Coffee: What's the Difference?

Cold brew and iced coffee look identical in a glass. But pour a sip of each and the difference is immediate — cold brew is smooth, low-acid, almost chocolatey; iced coffee is brighter, more acidic, and tastes like hot coffee that’s been chilled. They’re made completely differently, and knowing which is which changes how you make (and enjoy) your coffee at home. Here’s everything you need to know.
